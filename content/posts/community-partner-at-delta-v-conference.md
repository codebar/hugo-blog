*Our stand during the conference*

A huge thanks to team Delta V — [Jo](https://twitter.com/ThisIsJoFrank), [Perry](https://twitter.com/perrydyball) and [Simon](https://twitter.com/simonhearne) for their generous support and inclusion of codebar in their inaugural [conference](https://deltavconf.com/).

Over the 2 days we spoke with lots of people from all over the world about codebar and what we're doing. Our main aim during the conference was to spread the codebar mission and make people aware that we are not just London based, we have 18 others chapters too. Oh, and give people some stickers and notebooks too!

*[Kimberley Cook](https://twitter.com/KimberleyCook91) and [Oliver Turner](https://twitter.com/oliverturner) representing codebar*

We also loved meeting the other community partners; [Code Your Future](https://codeyourfuture.io/) and [Code Club](https://www.codeclub.org.uk/). Both of these organisations do wonderful things so if you've never heard of them we highly recommend clicking the above links and checking them out.

We were also able to sneak away from our stand to watch talks from Seren Davies, Leonie Watson, Harry Roberts and Henri Helvetica.

We are already excited for Delta V next year! 🎉
