The FT, one of the world’s leading news organisations, have been huge supporters of codebar for many years, first hosting one of our workshops all the way back in 2016. And now, we are so excited to reveal that we are deepening our partnership by welcoming them as a Gold sponsor!

![](https://cdn-images-1.medium.com/max/1024/1*0GhSuFr6LBJXN4W34he9HQ.jpeg)

Picture was taken from the last FT workshop

#### **What does this mean?**

A gold sponsor means that we are collaborating for 12 months. We will be working together on several one-off events for the community (both in-person and virtual), they will host several workshops for our [London](https://codebar.io/london) chapter, we will be sharing FT career opportunities, and they will be taking part in our virtual [job fair](https://hopin.com/events/codebar-job-fair-2023) on Monday 12th June.

Dewi Rees, Technical Director at the FT says *“I’m so pleased that the FT has shared my thinking around the value this relationship could bring with codebar. One of the reasons that I elected to join the FT in February 2022 was their commitment to* [*Diversity & Inclusivity*](https://aboutus.ft.com/company/diversity-inclusion)*- including sharing publicly their statistics and goals for each year. Along with the* [*FT values*](https://aboutus.ft.com/careers/values) *(most notably for me,* ***Ambition****,* ***Curiosity,*** *and* ***Inclusion****) and the culture I’ve observed while working here it really makes sense for us to directly partner and support codebar.”*

#### What’s next?

The FT’s next workshop is going to be hosted at Bracken House on July 5th, so if you are a codebar coach or student, keep an eye on the codebar website for that announcement. If you work at the FT, get in touch with Dewi or join the conversation on their internal Slack channels to hear about other opportunities too!

We’re ideally going to be running the first of our one-off events in the next couple of months and will share news of that on our website and socials. And they will be part of our job fair on 12th June so make sure you come along to hear all about career opportunities at the FT.

> It’s a real pleasure to partner with codebar in this way, it’s important to me to support charities like codebar and I know it’s also important to the FT.

If you’re interested in attending any of our events with the FT, make sure you are subscribed to the [London](https://codebar.io/london) chapter. Subscribing means you will receive email invites for our virtual and London based events. If you’re interested in chatting with people from the FT about career opportunities, come along to our [virtual job fair](https://hopin.com/events/codebar-job-fair-2023) on Monday 12th June from 6PM (BST).
